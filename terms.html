1. Description of Service

Hearth is a permission relay and supervision service for AI agents running on your personal computers. It forwards permission requests from AI software to your mobile device, allowing you to approve or deny actions remotely, and streams real-time activity data (actions taken, tool outputs, and transcript entries) to the app for your review.

Hearth does not execute commands, modify files, or control any AI agent directly. All actions taken on your systems are initiated by the AI software you have installed and configured. The Service relays and logs permission requests and your responses; it does not act on your systems independently.
